Brand Strategy & Visual Identity

Everything a brand does is informed by its strategy. It forms the foundation of the brand, defining who it is and why it exists. It comprises four key tenets: mission, vision, brand idea, and brand personality.

The visual identity is a set of visual elements that represent a brand and contribute to its recognition. It comprises the logo, color palette, typography, art direction, layouts, and design guidelines.
