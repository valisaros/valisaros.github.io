General FAQ’s

Account Management Questions

How can I change my password?

Access your Account Dashboard and go to the Password Settings Section where you can create a new password.

What happens if I have forgotten my password?

Ensure that the e-mail address given previously is accessible to date and we will be sending you a list of instructions to follow to reset your password.

Can I change my card details?

Yes you can - head over to your Account Dashboard > Payment Settings and update your card details.
My profile has not yet been verified what should I do?
Contact the support team here. However, do ensure that you have checked your Junk or Spam Emails prior to contacting us.

Can I delete or deactivate my account?

Yes you can, head over to your Account Dashboard > Deactivate Account
If you suspect that your account has been hacked, please contact us here to report the problem.
