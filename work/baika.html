Baika: Repairs Anytime, Anywhere.

Introducing Baika - where device repairs come to you. In the blink of an eye, simply make a request, and our skilled technicians will swiftly be dispatched to your doorstep, ensuring a hassle-free repair process.

The Baika experience extends beyond repairs, encompassing a cohesive brand expression that spans from logo and typography to illustration style and an impactful UI/UX product. Every element of the final system reflects the quality of our service, embodying its brand nature.

In development
Challenge

Imagine being able to request a technician right at your doorstep to repair your smartphone's broken glass. One of the primary challenges faced was to identify and implement a solution that is not only user-friendly and straightforward but also ensures accessibility for users of all ages, including the elderly.

Outcome

At the heart of a successful app lies a good user experience. A search bar on the homepage lets users initiate the process as soon as possible. The process consists of only four steps: Device search, choosing the problem, adding location, and selecting a payment method

Kicking things off with a solid brand strategy.

It all began with a discovery call to understand Baika’s needs and craft a custom plan tailored to help them achieve their goals.

The brand mission, vision, and personality work together to guide decisions and influence everything Baika does. They form the foundation of their brand, defining who they are and why they exist.

Empathise and UX Research Phase

The key to delivering a satisfying experience is understanding customers' needs and emotions.

After gaining insight into that, we constructed the information architecture and, based on that, developed user flows. The goal was to make the user journey as intuitive as possible.

Design Phase

Baika's design is a result of constant improvement and commitment. Iteration after iteration was made to achieve the final interface..

Brand guidelines, a style guide, wireframes, mid-fidelity, and high-fidelity designs were created.
